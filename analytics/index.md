# goBodhi Analytics & Metrics Desk

Understanding engagement levels in a decentralized voting space requires robust, manipulation-resistant metrics. The goBodhi engine calculates several metrics to compute hot feeds, voter rankings, and consistency streaks in real time.

---

## 📈 Score Calculators & Algorithms Catalog

Select a specialized metric node below to analyze the underlying mathematical formulations and triggers:

### 1. Deliberation Score Algorithm
- 🔥 **[Deliberation Score Tracking](./metrics/deliberation_score.md)** — Dynamic Heat formula:
  $$\text{Deliberation Score} = \frac{\text{Comment Count} \times \text{Average Comment Quality}}{\text{Active Hours}}$$
  Used to identify high-quality active debates rather than simple voting spikes.

### 2. Reputation Points Engine
- 🏆 **[User Reputation & Badges](./metrics/user_reputation.md)** — Points aggregation rule catalogs rewarding constructive activities (e.g. insightful suggestions, comment upvotes) while limiting sybil self-boosting.

### 3. Voting Streak Tracker
- ⚡ **[Voter Streak & Multiplier](./metrics/voting_streak.md)** — Calendar-consecutive calculations verifying daily engagement consistency, multiplier modifiers, and status updates.
